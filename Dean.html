Dean of Education & Student Affairs

Welcome to Delta University for Science and Technology, the university that was established in the delta region of the Arab Republic of Egypt as the first private university that works to serve its community and environment by reducing the alienation of its students and has established an important, basic, and objective principle that is the student is the center of the educational process. To achieve this principle, the university established the Deanship of Education and Student Affairs to organize the implementation of academic programs for university students completely and in accordance with the internal regulations of the faculties and to consolidate the principles of justice and transparency in students’ evaluation. Also, it organizes the student services provided by the university to its students and graduates and is the mainstay for supporting students in all activities. It also pays attention to building and developing their balanced personalities and works to enhance the skills and experiences that they will need when they set out for the labor market by providing digital and technological solutions that are authorized and documented for communication between students and graduates of the university and the labor market. It provides them with a modern and balanced technological environment within a framework of commitment to the decisions and laws of the country and is consistent with Egypt’s vision of 2030. Dr. Ahmed Rifaat Elshmouty Dean of Education and Student Affairs
